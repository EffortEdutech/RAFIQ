# QUL Provenance And Production Permission Request

Subject: Provenance and production-use rights for QUL resources 86, 88, 193, and 292

Assalamu alaikum,

We are developing RAFIQ, a Quran and Sunnah reflection and study application.
We downloaded and technically validated the following resources from QUL:

- `86`: QPC Hafs Ayah by Ayah
- `88`: Uthmani Ayah by Ayah
- `193`: Saheeh International English translation
- `292`: Abdullah Basamia Malay translation

The resource copyright pages currently do not provide sufficient
resource-specific copyright information for our production approval record.
Could you please provide, for each resource:

1. the original upstream source and exact edition/version
2. the author, translator, publisher, maintainer, and current rights holder
3. the applicable licence or permission document
4. whether commercial mobile and web application display is permitted
5. whether server storage, caching, search indexing, and user delivery are
   permitted
6. whether footnotes and metadata may be displayed
7. whether formatting without changing wording is permitted
8. whether embeddings, search metadata, or summaries are permitted
9. the exact required attribution and links
10. the correct contact if permission must be obtained from another party

For resource `292`, please also confirm whether `Abdullah Basamia` refers to
`Abdullah Muhammad Basmeih`, and identify the source publication.

For resources `86` and `88`, please identify the text/font upstream source and
any font or Quran-script-specific distribution conditions.

We will preserve the downloaded files verbatim, retain QUL resource IDs and
checksums, and will not publish the content until permission and attribution
are documented.
