| OFP-DEC-027 | Hadith navigation must be library-first on mobile before deeper practice mapping. | Approved | CP10A rebuilds `/hadith` around collections, selected collection, narration index, pagination, and narration-detail entry; chapter hierarchy will not be invented without backend metadata. |
| OFP-DEC-028 | Hadith detail must connect verification to practice without pretending every narration is a ruling. | Approved | CP10B adds Arabic original, preferred meaning fallback, Practice Map, Quran lens, careful action, boundary, and Hadith-scoped Related Sunnah Path. | OFP-DEC-029 | Hadith learning sessions must anchor the opened narration before showing related support. | Approved | CP10C adds `hadith_record` sessions, passes `hadithRecordId`, prepends the opened narration as first Sunnah support, dedupes related items, and routes the Sunnah learning step back to the narration. |
| OFP-DEC-030 | Guided knowledge must be the default journey; source browsing is secondary. | Approved | CP11 reframes `/search` as Guided Discovery and `/hadith` as Sunnah Practice. Users start from a life need or practice goal; source metadata and narration browsing stay available behind secondary controls. |
| OFP-DEC-031 | RAFIQ needs two search modes: Guidance Search and Source Search. | Approved | Guidance Search serves companionship and action. Source Search serves deeper research across Quran, translations, tafsir, Hadith, topics, themes, and verification. Guidance evidence must open deeper study; source results must be able to create a GuidanceSession. |
| OFP-DEC-032 | Source Search grouping and handoff belong in the backend contract, not only the UI. | Approved | CP12B adds `/api/private-content/search/sources` so mobile Source Search receives grouped, ranked, deep-linked results with source-to-guidance targets. | OFP-DEC-033 | Source Search deserves its own mobile route. | Approved | CP12C adds `/sources` so research is not hidden inside Guidance Search. `/search` remains guided companionship; `/sources` handles compact grouped source research and can hand selected results back into guidance. |
| OFP-DEC-034 | Guidance results must expose deeper study links. | Approved | CP12D adds shared guidance deep links across Today, Ask, Learn, and Sunnah Practice so Quran lens, tafsir, Sunnah support, and research suggestions are visible from the guidance result. |
| OFP-DEC-035 | Deep links must land on user-facing study rooms. | Approved | CP12E adds `/quran/:surah/:ayah` and upgrades narration detail so Quran lens, tafsir, Sunnah support, and source-search results land on study rooms before internal trust detail. |
| OFP-DEC-036 | CP13 mobile study-flow QA can pass while final Product Owner GO remains separate. | Approved | CP13 verifies the connected study journey and fixes source-density issues, but CP14 must polish Hadith reading weight, data-quality wording, translation coverage, and user-facing attribution before final GO. |
| OFP-DEC-037 | Attribution must be user-facing by default; internal provenance remains reviewer tooling. | Approved | CP14 rebuilds `/source-detail` around readable attribution, care status, and source names.
